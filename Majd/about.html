Welcome to Holy Family Co. Olive Wood Crafts!

Lovingly crafted from grade A olive wood Brothers Ashraf and Elias Jarayseh continue the tradition of Christian artefacts Olive Wood carving started by their great grandfather over 80 years ago.

Using time honoured turning, sawing and carving techniques each religious piece is lovingly created in the likeness of images, designed to evoke a spirituality and aesthetic appeal.


Their contemporary designs project the inherent artistic flair and crafting skills of the Palestinian people and their deep connection with the olive tree.

Lovingly crafted from grade A olive wood Hand made in Bethlehem where families carry on the age old tradition of crafting religious items from native olive wood.

No olive trees are cut solely for their wood, but off-cuts from the annual pruning and trimming trees are used.

Olive trees provide local people with oil for cooking, olives for eating, and wood for making beautiful objects.
